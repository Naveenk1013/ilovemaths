Correlation and Regression

Measure the relationship between two variables and use lines of best fit to predict the future.

Connecting the Dots

Correlation tells you IF two variables move together. Regression tells you exactly HOW they move together, allowing you to draw a line through a scatter plot.

$$ -1 \le r \le 1 $$
The Correlation Coefficient ($r$). 1 is a perfect positive relationship, -1 is perfect negative, and 0 means no relationship.
ConceptCorrelation vs Causation

Just because ice cream sales and shark attacks both go up in summer doesn't mean ice cream causes shark attacks!
