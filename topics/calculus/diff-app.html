Applications of Differential Calculus

Use derivatives to find peaks, valleys, rates of change, and optimize real-world problems.

Optimization

Calculus tells us exactly where a function reaches its highest or lowest points. At these peaks and valleys, the slope of the curve is perfectly flat.

$$ f'(x) = 0 $$
To find the maximum or minimum, take the derivative, set it to zero, and solve for $x$.
ConceptCritical Points

Points where $ f'(x) = 0 $ or is undefined.

TestSecond Derivative Test

Helps you determine if a point is a max or min.

If $ f''(x) > 0 $, it's a MINIMUM (concave up).
If $ f''(x) < 0 $, it's a MAXIMUM (concave down).
