Linear Programming

Maximize profits or minimize costs using constraints and graphical regions.

Optimization under Constraints

Linear programming helps you make the best decision in a mathematical model where requirements are represented by linear relationships.

Maximize $$ Z = ax + by $$
The Objective Function. This is what you want to make as large (or as small) as possible.
ConceptConstraints

The limits on your resources (like budget or materials).

$$ cx + dy \le E $$
$$ x \ge 0, y \ge 0 $$
VisualFeasible Region

The shaded area on a graph that satisfies all constraints.

The optimal solution ALWAYS lies at one of the corners (vertices) of this region.
